How are reports added

Reports are added automatically to the table when a user uses the Watcher Chrome Extension to send it.
Up^

How To Change the status of a report

When you want to change the status of a report simply click on the status symbol and it will be automatically changed to the next level. Avialble levels are: Pending, In Progress, Done and Not Rellevant.
Up^

How The Rank Is Calculated

The Rank is based on the amount of individual reports recieved for that cluster.
Reports are clustered according to thier URL address.
Up^

What The Sigma Stands For?

The total number of reports currently in the system (not including reports that have been deemed not relevant).
Up^

What The Pointer Stands For?

Precentage of pending reports out of all open reports (i.e. not marked as Done or Not Relevant).
Up^

What The Hourglass Stands For?

Average amount of time that a report was in pending state.
